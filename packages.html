Questions we get asked every day

Straight answers. No sales fluff.

Will my agent really be live in 48 hours?
Yes — and most launch faster. The moment your kickoff call ends our engineers start building. We handle everything: cloud provisioning, connector auth, testing, and final QA. You get a Loom walkthrough and a live handoff before we consider the job done. The 48-hour clock is a hard commitment, not a marketing claim.
Do I need a developer or IT team on my side?
Not at all. We built AETHER specifically for business owners and operators — not engineers. You'll answer a few plain-English questions during onboarding, then we disappear and build. No code, no config files, no internal IT resources required. If you can use Gmail, you can run an AETHER agent.
What if the agent makes a mistake or goes off-script?
Every agent ships with a policy guardrail layer that defines exactly what it can and cannot do autonomously. Anything outside those bounds gets flagged and held for human review. On top of that, Catalyst includes business-hours human monitoring and Momentum runs 24/7 oversight plus self-healing — so the agent corrects itself before you even notice an issue.
How do you keep my business data secure?
All tool connections use secure OAuth — we never touch your passwords or store credentials in plain text. Data in transit is encrypted with TLS 1.3; at rest we use AES-256. Your agent runs in a dedicated cloud container isolated from every other customer. Momentum customers can optionally deploy into a private VPC on their own infrastructure.
Can I cancel if it's not right for my business?
Catalyst and Momentum are month-to-month — cancel any time with 30 days' notice, no penalty, no questions. Catalyst also carries a 30-day money-back guarantee: if the agent doesn't deliver measurable value in the first month, we refund you in full. Spark is a 6-month commitment (locked-in price, not locked-in results) with the same money-back window on month one.
My industry is regulated (law / healthcare / finance). Can you handle that?
Yes. We've designed AETHER agents around regulated workflows specifically. The agent handles intake, qualification, scheduling, follow-up, and document triage — the licensed professional makes every regulated call. HIPAA deployments require a Business Associate Agreement (BAA), which we'll help you set up.
How is this different from ChatGPT, Copilot, or HubSpot's AI?
Those are tools — they reply when you ask them something. AETHER builds agents — they decide what to do, take action across your stack (CRM, calendar, email, Slack), and handle entire workflows end-to-end without you in the loop. Completely different product category.
How will I know the agent is actually working?
Momentum customers get a real-time command-center dashboard showing every action the agent takes, queued tasks, and outcomes. Catalyst customers receive weekly performance summaries via email plus full audit logs on request. And because the agent is doing things in your actual tools — booking meetings, sending emails, updating CRMs — the results are instantly visible where your work already lives.
